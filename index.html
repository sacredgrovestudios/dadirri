Avro
EARTH FORM

Avro’s Earth form is representative of a combination of the fantasy land of Dadirri and of Earth - as it is mix between a faun and a human. Avro differs from other Dadirrians and his unique appearance sets him apart from the rest as he is the chosen one - the guardian entrusted with the fate of Dadirri and Earth.

Avro
WATER FORM

Avro’s water form is granted during the game as he interacts with the koi fish deity within the water temple. Avro gains the ability to transform into a pufferfish which allows him to navigate the waters and travel through water and float on top of rushing water in order to repair the temple before Dadirri and Earth are destroyed.

Avro
FIRE FORM

Avro’s fire form is granted inside the fire temple. After interacting with the dragon fire deity, he obtains the ability to transform into a fiery armadillo that can smash into walls and roll through small spaces. This form is quite durable and protects Avro against hot and harsh environmental conditions found in the temples.

Dadirri
THE ISLANDS

The land of Dadirri is made up of floating islands that are accessed via rope bridges. The main island of Dadirri contains a small village that is the home to Avro and all the Dadirrian people. Further away, inside the great Tree of Life, lives the village Elder

The main island acts as a hub for all Dadirrians to navigate to the surrounding islands. A mysterious hole in the middle of the island has developed and pieces of land are crumbling to Earth below. Whispers among the villagers speak of a link between the elemental temples and the strange environmental events occuring in Dadirri.

Dadirri
THE TEMPLES

One specific island is home to sacred temples, each linked to a natural element - water and fire. The deities worshipped by Dadirrians of the past - the water spirit embodied in a koi fish, and the fire spirit, embodied in a dragon, have been long forgotten. As such, the magic in the temples have faded and the temples have fallen into disrepair.

Legends tell of great power bestowed by the elemental dieties upon a worthy Dadirrian - the Guardian. Only the Guardian can repair the fantastical machinery held within the temples and save Dadirri and Earth.
